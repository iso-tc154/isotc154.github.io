ISO/TC 154 is pleased to announce the appointment of
link:{{ pan_wei.url }}[{{ pan_wei.name }}] ({{ pan_wei.affiliation }})
as the new Chairperson of the committee, effective immediately.

Ms. Pan brings extensive experience in standardization and international
cooperation to the role. She will lead ISO/TC 154 in its ongoing mission
to develop and maintain international standards for processes, data
elements and documents in commerce, industry and administration.

Ms. Pan succeeds Mr. Shi Yu, who served as Chair from 2021 to 2025.
The committee expresses its sincere gratitude to Mr. Shi Yu for his
dedicated leadership and significant contributions during his tenure.
